ABOUT

About Us? No, It's all about you

Nitro drills was established on 1991 with the goal to deliver the perfect tools for daily and professional use of our customers. At first it was named Nitro Tools. But upon huge demand and popularity of our drills we decided to focus only on one type of product, so that we can serve our customers with more quality and unique technology. We renamed our company as Nitro Drills on 1994 and successfully serving our customers with the best drills since then. Our main focus is customer satisfaction and happiness. We believe customers are always right. With that in mind we make our products with extreme care and keeping all sort of customers in mind. We take notes from our customers to improve our service and also what they need/want. By doing so we can adapt to their needs and full-fill their requirements.
